Zenobia Johnson is currently a Senior at the University of New Haven
working on her Bachelor of Arts in Music and Sound Recording with a minor in English.

She is a member of Sigma Tau Delta, the English National Honor's Society,
president of WRITE Poetry Club, Sergeant at Arms of the Internationl Student Association,
and a corresponding writer of the Charger Bulletin.

She has recorded for on campus bands such as On the Fritz and Crystal Clear for school
assignments. Has recorded original song for class assignment titled "Where Did the Day Go?".
